Badge System Guidelines

Overview

The badge system categorizes student achievements into four levels based on their proficiency in a course:

  1. Platinum
  2. Gold
  3. Silver
  4. Bronze

Each badge represents the student's mastery of a course, with Platinum being the highest level and Bronze the entry level. Badges are awarded based on a combination of course completion, assessment scores, participation, and overall engagement.

Badge Awarding Process

  1. Automatic Evaluation: The system will track course progress, quiz scores, and participation to automatically award badges.
  2. Progress Indicators: Students will see their progress towards the next badge level on their dashboard.
  3. Notifications: When a student earns a badge, they will receive a congratulatory message along with tips on how to reach the next level.

Badge Display & Benefits

  • Dashboard Showcase: Badges will be displayed on the student's profile page for peers to view.
  • Skill Certificates: For Gold and Platinum badges, students can download certificates to showcase on LinkedIn or other platforms.
  • Leaderboard: Students who consistently earn Platinum badges may be featured on the portal's leaderboard, motivating others to excel.

Additional Notes

  • Badge Expiration: Badges do not expire, but students can retake courses to upgrade their badge level.
  • Badge Criteria Updates: Criteria may be adjusted periodically based on student feedback and course difficulty to ensure fair assessments.
  • Special Badges: Occasionally, special badges can be awarded for completing challenges, participating in events, or contributing to the community.
